CASA AR – Augmented Reality Home Visualization

We have built CASA AR, an augmented reality app that redefines how users design and visualize their homes. It offers a two-phase experience—first, by converting 2D floor plans into interactive 3D models for real-time spatial planning, and second, by enabling a full AR walkthrough with customizable furniture and real-time cost estimation. CASA AR helps users make smarter, more confident design choices by visualizing their future home before construction begins.

AR Measurement Tape & Paint Estimation Tool

We have developed an AR-based wall measurement and paint estimation solution using Unity, designed to make home improvement tasks faster and more accurate. This intuitive application allows users to measure wall dimensions in real time and estimate the amount of paint required with precision. By leveraging augmented reality, we’ve streamlined the process, reduced manual errors, and delivered a more efficient way to plan and execute interior projects.
